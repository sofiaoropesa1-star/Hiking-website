Introduction to Hiking

Hiking is walking through forests, mountains, deserts and other natural places. It improves your physical health, reduces stress, and connects you with nature.

How to Prepare for a Hike

Before hiking, check the weather, research the trail, bring water, snacks, and a first-aid kit. Wear good shoes and tell someone where you are going.

Simplified Summary

  • Check weather
  • Pack water and snacks
  • Wear strong shoes

What To Do If You Get Lost

If you get lost, stay calm. Stop moving, think, observe your surroundings, and plan. Make yourself visible and call for help.

Simplified Summary

  • Stop moving
  • Stay calm and think
  • Call or signal for help

Dangers of Hiking

Watch for fast-changing weather, dehydration, and uneven ground. Always stay alert and avoid risky shortcuts.

Simplified Summary

  • Weather changes quickly
  • You need to stay hydrated
  • Ground can be dangerous
