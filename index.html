Introduction to Hiking

Hiking means walking through forests, mountains, deserts, and parks. Anyone can enjoy it without special skills.

It strengthens your body, reduces stress, and lets you experience nature.

Mountain Hiking

  • High altitude
  • Steep climbs
  • Cooler air at the top

Canyon Trails

  • Steep rocky walls
  • Cooler shaded paths
  • Famous in the Southwest U.S.

Forest Trails

  • Lush green surroundings
  • Soft dirt pathways
  • Great shade on sunny days

How to Prepare

Research your trail, check weather, dress in layers, and pack essentials like water and snacks.

Dangers of Hiking

Weather can change quickly, wildlife may appear, and dehydration can happen. Stay alert and follow safety rules.

What to Do If You Get Lost

Stay calm and use the STOP method: Stop, Think, Observe, Plan. Make yourself visible and signal for help.
