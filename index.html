Introduction to Hiking

Hiking is walking through natural places such as forests, mountains and deserts. It helps fitness, reduces stress, and connects you with nature.

Quick summary

  • Good exercise
  • Low cost to start
  • Bring water and good shoes

How to Prepare

Research the trail, check weather, wear layers, pack water, snacks and a small first-aid kit. Tell someone your plan.

What to Do If You Get Lost

If you get lost, stop, stay calm, use the STOP method (Stop, Think, Observe, Plan), make yourself visible, and signal for help.

Quick summary

  • Stop moving
  • Make yourself visible
  • Use whistle or phone if available

Dangers of Hiking

Watch weather, wildlife, dehydration and rough terrain. Take breaks and avoid risky shortcuts.

Quick summary

  • Weather can change fast
  • Stay hydrated
  • Watch your footing
