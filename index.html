Introduction to Hiking

Hiking is a wonderful activity that lets you explore forests, mountains, and beautiful landscapes.

It's great for your body, mind, and spirit.

Mountain Hiking

Mountain hikes offer steep climbs, amazing views, and cooler temperatures at high altitudes.

You’ll encounter rocky slopes, strong winds, and breathtaking scenery.

Canyon Trails

Canyons create deep narrow pathways carved by nature over millions of years.

You’ll find steep rocky walls, shaded routes, and incredible echo sounds.

Forest Trails

Forests are full of life and peaceful scenery.

You’ll experience wildlife, shade, soft ground, and fresh air.

How to Prepare

Always bring water, food, maps, and appropriate clothing.

Check weather and trail conditions before starting your journey.

Dangers of Hiking

Sudden weather changes, dehydration, and wildlife can be dangerous if you're unprepared.

If You Get Lost

Use the S.T.O.P method: Stop, Think, Observe, Plan.

Stay visible, stay calm, and stay in one place until help arrives.
